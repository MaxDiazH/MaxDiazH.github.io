Course Policies

1. Enrollment and Payment

1.1 Payment is required to participate in the course. Classes are charged at $10 each, or $30 per month if paid monthly.

1.2 Intentional avoidance of payment will result in removal from the Zulip group and loss of access to class materials and sessions.

1.3 Late payments will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis, but regular delays may lead to termination of enrollment.

2. Content and Intellectual Property

2.1 Course materials, including lectures, assignments, and problem sets, are intended solely for enrolled students. Redistribution or resale of any content is strictly prohibited.

2.2 Unauthorized sharing of any part of the course (including recordings, slides, or notes) will lead to immediate removal from the course.

3. Classroom Etiquette

3.1 Respectful behavior is expected from all participants during lectures and in the Zulip group. Disruptive behavior may lead to removal from the course.

3.2 To maintain a positive and focused learning environment, please avoid off-topic conversations in the Zulip group.

4. Attendance and Participation

4.1 Regular attendance and active participation are encouraged to get the most from the course.

4.2 Classes will not be recorded unless otherwise specified. If you miss a class, it is your responsibility to catch up on missed material.

5. Privacy and Communication

5.1 Student information will be used solely for course purposes and will not be shared with third parties.

5.2 For any concerns or questions, please contact us directly through our designated communication channels.

6. Course Changes

6.1 The course structure, schedule, and content may be subject to adjustments. Students will be informed of any significant changes in advance.

6.2 The organizer reserves the right to cancel classes if necessary. In such cases, affected students will be compensated or rescheduled as appropriate.

7. Grievance Policy

7.1 If you have concerns or complaints, please address them directly with the course organizer.

7.2 We aim to maintain a respectful and open environment for resolving issues.

8. Terms of Dismissal

8.1 Severe or repeated breaches of the policies outlined may lead to immediate dismissal.
