Math Olympiad Training

If you are looking to prepare for the National Mathematics Olympiad, we offer group training sessions designed to develop problem-solving skills and logical reasoning. During our sessions, we will work with the specific material required for the Olympiads, covering areas such as algebra, geometry, combinatorics, number theory, and analysis.

FAQ

What courses do you currently offer?

Currently, we are offering an introduction to proofs oriented to math olympiad problem solving, in a nutshell all you need to begin understanding mathematics olympiad correctly.

When does the course take place?

The course runs from April to October, aligning with the National Mathematics Olympiad schedule to prepare students for the test.

How are the classes structured?

We use the Zulip chat platform to share course materials and problem sets, allowing students to discuss and collaborate. Each week, we hold an online lecture to cover next week’s topics.
